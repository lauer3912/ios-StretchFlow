# StretchGoGo - App Store Listing

## App Name
**StretchGoGo** - Daily Stretching & Yoga Companion

## Description
Transform your daily routine with StretchGoGo – your personal stretching and yoga companion designed for busy professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone seeking better flexibility and wellness.

### Key Features:
- **Guided Stretch Sessions**: Follow expert-designed stretching routines with clear timer-based guidance
- **Targeted Workouts**: Focus on specific body parts – upper body, lower body, back & spine, neck & shoulders, hip flexors
- **Session Variety**: Choose from 5 to 30-minute sessions across Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced levels
- **Progress Tracking**: Build healthy habits with streak tracking and calendar heatmaps
- **Achievement System**: Unlock badges as you progress – first session, 7-day streak, 30-day streak, and more
- **Dark & Light Themes**: Beautiful design optimized for day and night use
